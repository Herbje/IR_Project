A pub /p åb/ , formally public house (a house open to the public, as opposed to a private house), is a drinking establishment in Britain, Ireland, New Zealand, Australia, Canada and Denmark.raditionally the windows of town pubs were of smoked or frosted glass to obscure the clientele from the street but from the 1990s onwards, there has been a move towards clear glass, in keeping with brighter interiors. The owner, tenant or manager (licensee) of a pub is properly known as the pub landlord.
