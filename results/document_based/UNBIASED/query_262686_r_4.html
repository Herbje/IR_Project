Query: how long is a operational tour - Docno: 8243359


A SEAL officer typically, goes straight to a SEAL Team or SDV Team for first operational tour and does 2 years of training, workups, and overseas deployment. If first assignment is a SDV Team, second tour will be on a SEAL Team. On first tour, SEAL officer will be an Assistant Platoon Commander in an Assistant Officer-in-Charge (AOIC) billet. Second 2-year operational tour, SEAL officer will be a Platoon Leader in an Officer-in-Charge billet.
