Where the Sidewalk Ends (book). From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. For the 1950 film, see Where the Sidewalk Ends. Where the Sidewalk Ends (1974) is a collection of children's poetry written and illustrated by Shel Silverstein and published by Harper and Row Publishers. The book's poems address many common childhood concerns and also presents purely fanciful stories.
