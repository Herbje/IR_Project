'Diana was pregnant when she died - but Dodi was not the father' claims French journalist. By IAN SPARKS. Last updated at 15:59 23 August 2007. Princess Diana was almost certainly nine to ten weeks pregnant when she died, it has been claimed. French investigative journalist Chris Laffaille says he uncovered evidence of the pregnancy from official archives of the Paris hospital where the princess was taken after the crash on the night of August 31, 1997.
