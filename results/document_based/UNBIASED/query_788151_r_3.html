To request a copy of the PUB 13 in large print, Braille, or audio CD, contact the Civil Rights Bureau at: crb@dss.ca.gov. The PUB 13 pamphlets are available in all waiting rooms and reception areas and are distributed and explained to all applicants and recipients upon intake and reinvestigation of eligibility.he pamphlet: 1 spells out your rights as an applicant/recipient. 2 specifies that multilingual services are available. 3 lists the TDD (Telecommunication Device for the Deaf). 4 explains the steps you can take if you do not agree with the action of your application or services. 5 explains the State Hearings process.
