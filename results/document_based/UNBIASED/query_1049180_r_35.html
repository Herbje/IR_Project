stroopwafel In the event on an exposure, you have the opportunity to avert an infection with a 28-day course of HIV post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP). The timing of PEP has been shown to be more effective the sooner it is started:preferably less than 6 hours after the exposure but no more than 72 hours at the latest.
